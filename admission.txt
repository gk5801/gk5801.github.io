<h2><strong>NUS florida Application Process 2020</strong></h2>
<p>Candidates can apply online or offline for admissions to UG/PG courses at National University of Schooling according to their convenience.</p>
<p><strong>Online Mode:</strong> Applicants can apply directly through the application form available at the official website of NUS. They need to fill
in all the mandatory details such as&nbsp;marks obtained in matric and high school, other educational details and type, of course, they want to apply for and 
also they need to mention personal details such as name, contact number and email id.</p><p>After completing the registration, the query will be directed
to admission cell. The admission counselor will call you for further process.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Offline Mode: </strong>Candidates need to visit the NUS campus to apply for any UG/PG course before the last date of registration. 
They have to visit the campus with the original documents and need to fill in the application form with all the necessary details and provide all the 
documents required.</p><h4><strong>

<ol>
<li>Valid NET score card mentioning the rank attained by the candidate.</li>
<li>Provisional Degree in the case of applying for any of the PG programs.</li>
<li>Diploma certificate, if applying for 2nd-year admissions to any of the UG programs.</li>
<li>matrix and high school mark sheets and cetificates.</li>
</ol>
</p>
